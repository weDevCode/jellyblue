HTML Structure

If you want to customize your theme, you must see this first. The HTML is the HyperText Markup Language. It's very easy to customize the content of HTML

For example: <p>awesome content is here</p>

The content between p tag can changeable if you want

Almost HTML tag's content can changeable, but some tag aren't

For example: <!DOCTYPE html>. This is a HTML declaration tag (ver5)

The title and meta tags

Let's focus at in the above image. The title tag is very important for the website. It can tell your customer about your awesome website. It's maybe your brand name or your company name.

Next is the meta tag. The meta tag is very important as the title tag. It can tell the search engine bots or social network bot to index your site to they search engine or they platform. So it's very important that you can not ignore them.

Some infomation about these tags:

  • og:title: your own title
  • og:site_name: your own site name
  • og:image: your image link to website to display on social network
  • og:description: description about your site on facebook
  • og:url: your website url
  • description: your site description on search engine
  • author: Your name or your IT employee

Facebook app id tag

  • fb:admins: your facebook id
  • fb:app_id: your facebook app id

Twitter Meta tag

  • twitter:site: your twitter username
  • twitter:creator: your twitter username (second)
  • twitter:description: Your website description
  • twitter:title: Your website url
  • twitter:image:src: Your image link to this website
